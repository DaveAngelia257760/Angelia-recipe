Killer Pork Sinigang

Sinigang is a sour soup native to the Philippines. This recipe uses pork as the main ingredient.

  • Ingredients

  • 2lb prok belly
  • 5 oz Daikon radish
  • 8 pieces Okra
  • 1 piece Onion
  • 18 pieces String beans
  • 2 pieces Tomato
  • 1 Fish suace and ground pepper
  • 66 g Knorr sinigang sa sampaloc mix with gabi
  • 6 pieces Shishito Pepper
  • 3 tbsp Cooking oil
  • 8 cups of water
  • 1 bunch Kangkong
  • 2 pieces Talong(sliced)
