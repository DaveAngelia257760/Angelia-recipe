Fried Chicken with Mayo Garlic Glaze

Make the Mayo Garlic glaze by melting butter on a clean pan. Add garlic. Cook until it starts to brown.

  • Ingredients

  • 1 ½ lb Chicken thighs
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • ½ cup Lady's choice mayonnaise
  • ½ tsp lemon juice
  • ¾ cup All-Purpose flour
  • ½ tsp Black pepper,ground
  • ½ cup cornstarch
  • 2 ⅓ tbsp Knorr liquid seasoning
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • 2 cups cooking oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
