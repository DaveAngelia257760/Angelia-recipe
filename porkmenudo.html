Filipino Pork Menudo

Quick and Easy Menudo recipe that your family will surely love. Try our recipe now!

  • Ingredients

  • 3 pieces Hotdogs
  • 1 piece Knorr pork cube
  • 4 oz Pork liver
  • 2 lb Pork shoulder
  • 1 piece Baking potato
  • 2 pieces Bay leaves, dried
  • 1 piece Carrot
  • 4 cloves Garlic
  • 1 tsp Ginger, ground
  • ½ cup Raisins
  • 1 piece Yellow onion
  • 2 tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1 can Tomato sauce
  • 1 Salt and ground black pepper
  • 3 tbsp Cooking oil
