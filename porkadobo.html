Pork Adobo

Pork Adobo is pork cooked in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic.It is considered as the Philippine's national dish because of its popularity.

  • Ingredients

  • 2 ½ lb pork belly
  • 4 peices bay leaves,dried
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 3 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 tsp peppercorn, whole
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 10 tbsp white vinegar
  • 12 ox cola drink
  • 1 cup water
