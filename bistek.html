Bistek Tagalog (Filipino Beef Steak)

Bistek Tagalog is made of thinly sliced sirloin braised in a mixture of citrus, soy sauce, onions, and garlic. This Filipino beef steak is hearty, tasty, and perfect with steamed rice.

  • Ingredients

  • 600 g Sirloin
  • 5 Onions
  • ½ cup Soy Sauce
  • 1 pinch pepper
  • 5 tbsp Cooking oil
  • ½ cup Water
  • 1 Fish suace and ground pepper
  • 4 tbsp Calamansi Juice
  • Patis to taste
